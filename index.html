First Class Care For Your Pet

Our aim is to provide first class, stress-free cat accommodation which acts as a home away from home.

Boarding cats in a friendly, secure environment. You may visit our cattery at any time during normal business hours to view our bespoke, cat accommodations.

Please take a look around our site to find out more about us and what we offer – you are more than welcome to visit or contact us to discuss your pet’s individual requirements.

Please check the Terms & Conditions before booking.

 

Interaction

Each cat is attended to daily!

This entails a basic groom, check-over and lots of attention. Classical music will be played throughout the establishement. All to ensure they enjoy a comfortable and relaxing stay.

 

Nutrition

Your cat's health is our top priority.

We offer only the best natural food - preferring to use Iams or Royal Canin dry food and nature diet fresh food. We can cater for most diets from our selection of foods, alternately you are welcome to bring your own food.

We even offer an à la carte menu of freshly cooked fish or chicken each day for an extra charge of £2 per day.
