When can I drop off and collect my cats?

You can check- in and collect your animal any time WITHIN our opening hours.

Opening Hours: Monday to Saturday 09:00 – 17:00 Sunday and Bank Holidays 09:00 – 11:30

Please be aware that we do not have an option for out of hours check -in/out.

What if my cat is on medication?

We are happy to give medication, as per your instructions. Please mention at the time of booking to confirm we can cater for their requirements. There is no extra charge for giving medication.

We will also have your cat checked over by our on-site vet if we have any concerns.

Can I cancel my booking at any time?

We require a minimum of 7 days notice for cancellations and reserve the right to charge up to the full amount if this is not given. All days booked are chargeable, unless previously agreed

Do I need an appointment to view

You may come and view our cattery at any time during our open hours. Please make us aware beforehand so we know to expect your arrival.
